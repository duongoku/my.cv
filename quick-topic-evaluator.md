# Quick Topic Evaluator

## 60-Second Test: Is This a Research-Grade PhD Topic?

### ⚡ Question 1: What is the KNOWLEDGE GAP?
*One sentence: What don't we know that your research will reveal?*

❌ **Bad Answer:** "We don't have a good memory assistant app"
✅ **Good Answer:** "We don't know how long-term AI memory assistance affects human cognitive abilities"

---

### ⚡ Question 2: What is the THEORETICAL CONTRIBUTION?
*One sentence: What scientific understanding will your research advance?*

❌ **Bad Answer:** "A working system that people can use"
✅ **Good Answer:** "A theoretical framework explaining when AI assistance enhances vs. atrophies human cognition"

---

### ⚡ Question 3: Where would this publish?
*Specific venues: CHI, IMWUT, CSCW, AAAI, etc.*

❌ **Bad Answer:** "TechCrunch, Product Hunt, Hacker News"
✅ **Good Answer:** "CHI (HCI), IMWUT (Ubicomp), CSCW (social computing), AAAI (AI)"

---

### ⚡ Question 4: Is the system a MEANS or an END?
*Purpose of building anything*

❌ **Bad Answer:** "The system IS the contribution - it helps people"
✅ **Good Answer:** "The system is a tool to collect data and answer research questions"

---

## Scoring Rubric

| Criteria | ❌ Product | ⚠️ Borderline | ✅ Research |
|----------|------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Main output** | Working system | System + some findings | Theoretical insights |
| **Novelty** | Features, UX | New context for existing theory | New theory/framework |
| **Publications** | Blog posts, demos | Workshop papers | Top-tier conferences |
| **Success measure** | Users, performance | Users + some insights | Knowledge advancement |
| **Thesis title** | "Building X" | "X and its effects on Y" | "Understanding Y through X" |

---

## Red Flag Checklist

🔴 **STOP - This is a product, not research:**
- [ ] Pitch sounds like startup idea
- [ ] Success = "people will use this"
- [ ] Can't state specific research questions
- [ ] Would fit on Product Hunt more than CHI

🟡 **CAUTION - Needs reframing:**
- [ ] Mainly building system, but some interesting questions
- [ ] Need to shift focus from "does it work" to "what does it reveal"
- [ ] Can be rescued by emphasizing research over engineering

🟢 **GO - This is research:**
- [ ] Clear research questions
- [ ] Theoretical contribution obvious
- [ ] System is tool for data collection
- [ ] Top venue publication potential

---

## Instant Transformations

### Product → Research in 3 Steps

**Step 1: Identify the system**
- Example: "AI memory assistant"

**Step 2: Ask "So what?"**
- Example: "Why does this matter? What effects does it have?"

**Step 3: Formulate research question**
- Example: "How does AI memory assistance affect natural memory formation?"

---

## Examples: Before and After

| ❌ Product Pitch | ✅ Research Topic |
|-----------------|------------------|
| "Build an app that helps people remember things" | "How does AI-mediated memory recall affect people's natural memory abilities?" |
| "Create an AI tutor for students" | "When does AI tutoring scaffold vs. hinder learning? A study of metacognitive development" |
| "Make a wearable that tracks attention" | "What are the privacy boundaries around biosignal monitoring in workplace contexts?" |
| "Develop a system to improve sleep" | "How does sensory stimulation during sleep affect next-day cognitive performance in natural settings?" |

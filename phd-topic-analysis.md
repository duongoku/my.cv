# PhD Topic Feasibility Analysis for 3-Year Timeline

## Topics to RULE OUT (Require 4+ years)

### ❌ Topic 1: Early Detection & Intervention for Cognitive Decline
**Why it's too long:**
- Requires **longitudinal validation**: To prove you can detect "early cognitive decline," you need to follow participants for 1-2+ years to see who actually declines
- Need large sample sizes to get enough people who decline during study period
- Ethical issues: What do you do when you detect decline? Can't just observe without intervention
- Example: Current AD studies track people for 3-5+ years

**Better for:** Postdoc, research institute with ongoing longitudinal studies

---

### ❌ Topic 5: Voice-Based Cognitive Health Monitoring
**Why it's too long:**
- Same issue as Topic 1: Validating that voice changes predict cognitive decline requires longitudinal follow-up
- Need to collect baseline data, then wait to see who declines
- Would need 2-3 years minimum of data collection + analysis

**Better for:** Collaboration with labs that have existing longitudinal datasets

---

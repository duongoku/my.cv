## SPECIFIC RESEARCH QUESTIONS I'M INTERESTED IN

Please explore these directions and identify which have **genuine research gaps** (not just engineering opportunities):

1. **Cognitive Offloading:**
   - What are the long-term effects of offloading memory/tasks to AI?
   - Does cognitive offloading lead to **cognitive atrophy** or **cognitive freedom** for higher-level thinking?
   - How do we measure and optimize this balance?

2. **Just-in-Time AI Interventions:**
   - What makes AI interventions **helpful** vs. **annoying** in daily life?
   - How do we detect **optimal moments** for AI assistance?
   - **Individual differences** in receptivity to AI interventions

3. **Human-AI Collaborative Sensemaking:**
   - How do people and AI **work together** to understand complex information?
   - What AI capabilities **enhance human sensemaking** vs. **replace it**?
   - **Explainability for sensemaking** - what explanations help?

4. **Personalization vs. Serendipity:**
   - Personalized AI can create **filter bubbles** and **reduced exposure** to diversity
   - How do we design AI that personalizes **while preserving serendipity**?
   - Trade-offs between **efficiency and exploration**

5. **Privacy, Trust, and Dependence:**
   - How do people develop **trust in personal AI** over time?
   - When does helpful AI become **problematic dependence**?
   - **Privacy paradox** - people want personalized AI but are uncomfortable with data collection

6. **AI for Learning and Skill Acquisition:**
   - How does AI tutoring affect **long-term retention and transfer**?
   - **Scaffolding vs. crutch** - when does AI help vs. hinder learning?
   - **Metacognitive awareness** - how do people learn about their own learning with AI?
